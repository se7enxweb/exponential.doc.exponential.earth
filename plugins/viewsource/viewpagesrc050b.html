Welcome to the Technical Documentation of The Exponential CMS 5.x

This documentation covers functionality in Exponential 5.x, a hybrid version of Exponential CMS containing Exponential 4.x "Legacy", and "Platform" a new generation of Exponential on top of Symfony 2 Full stack framework. For further reading on the difference between Platform and legacy, and how they are both bundled in Exponential 5.x versions, see Exponential 5 Architecture - Introduction & Overview.
