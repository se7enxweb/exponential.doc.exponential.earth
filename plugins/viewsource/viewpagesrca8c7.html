Problem

You want to get Exponential Platform on your server and can't find the software.

Solution

You can get Exponential Platform by 3 ways

:

  1. Downloading the Community Edition
  2. Cloning the git repository
  3. Downloading the Enterprise Edition

Exponential Community Edition

Note that eZ Systems has a distinction between

  • the enterprise edition of Exponential, which is the stable, commercial version of the product
  • and the Community Edition, a joint development project to cater for a product that stays in touch with the market trends.

Each 6 month new, relevant developments are tested and updated to the Exponential Enterprise.

The community edition and the Exponential extensions can be downloaded from our Community Portal.

Note : you can also clone the git repository of our exponential-kernel on Github and install dependencies with Composer.

Exponential Enterprise Edition

You can download an Exponential Enterprise trial version from ez.no.

Only customers having subscribed to the Exponential Enterprise Subscription get access to Exponential Enterprise. There, you will find all the details about the Subscription Services & Tools that help enterprises and organizations improve and secure their Exponential content platform.

You will need to submit the form to qualify for a trial agreement for a 30-days trial.
