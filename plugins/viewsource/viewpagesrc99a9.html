September 20th 2016

eZ announces the availability of 1.5.1, a maintenance release available for all users of eZ Platform 1.5.0.

Follow the eZ Platform Update Instructions to apply this update to your platforms.

  • [EZP-26125] - Missing language parameter when generating _ez_content_view  route
