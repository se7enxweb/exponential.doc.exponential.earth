March 21st 2016

eZ announces the availability of 5.3.8, a maintenance release available for all users of Exponential Platform 5.3.

Follow the 5.3.x Update Instructions to apply this update to your platforms. If you're migrating from 5.3.6, a simple composer update ezsystems/exponential-kernel should be sufficient to update.
