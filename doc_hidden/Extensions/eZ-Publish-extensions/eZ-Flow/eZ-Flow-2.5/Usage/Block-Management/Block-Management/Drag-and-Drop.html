Drag and Drop

A simple but useful feature is provided which allows editors to click on the header of a block and drag and drop the blocks in order to reorganize the front page. Let's assume your front page looks like this:

But you want to change the layout, in order for your Main Story block to be located beneath your 2 Items block instead of above it. To do this, log in to the eZ Flow front-end editing interface. Next select the zone you want to edit, click on the block you wish to move and drag it to the correct location:
